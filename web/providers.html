Frequently Asked Questions

How are earnings calculated? +
Earnings are based on the number of tokens processed, your GPU performance, uptime, and network demand. Premium models and faster response times earn higher rates. Payments are processed automatically every week.
What about electricity costs? +
Most providers earn significantly more than their electricity costs. A typical RTX 3080 uses about 300W under load, costing roughly $0.05-0.10 per hour. With average earnings of $1.50-2.50 per hour, you'll have a healthy profit margin.
Can I limit GPU usage? +
Yes! You can set custom limits for GPU usage, operating hours, and maximum power consumption. The provider software includes advanced scheduling options to run only during specific times or when your computer is idle.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. All inference requests are encrypted end-to-end. The provider software runs in an isolated environment and only processes AI model inference - it cannot access your personal files or data. We also support privacy-preserving inference modes for sensitive workloads.
What are the minimum payouts? +
The minimum payout threshold is $10, which most active providers reach within a few days. Payments are processed weekly via cryptocurrency (ETH, USDC) or monthly via traditional payment methods (PayPal, bank transfer).
